IBM HR Analytics

IBM with around 1400 employees is experiencing employee attrition. Employee attrition occurs as employees retire or resign and are not replaced. Employee attrition can be costly for business. The company loses employee productivity, and employee knowledge. The original dataset contains of 35 variables (numerical & categorical) which are ranging from personal information, work experience related, work environment, work benefit, etc.
